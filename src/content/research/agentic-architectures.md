# Agentic Architectures on M-Series Chips

The modern MacBook Pro (specifically M3/M4/M5 Max series) has fundamentally shifted the baseline for decentralized AI. With unified memory architectures, we are no longer constrained by the VRAM bottlenecks of traditional PCIe GPUs.

## Running 8B Models Locally

Using tools like **LM Studio** and **Ollama**, we can successfully run models like `Llama-3-8B-Instruct` embedded directly into our daily workflows. 

### Why Decentralize?
1. **Privacy**: Research data never leaves the local machine.
2. **Latency**: Zero network overhead for rapid reasoning loops.
3. **Autonomy**: Your digital brain (the agent) remains functionally alive even when disconnected from the grid.
